About Electricians-SA & Plumbers

Your trusted partner for over 20 years of excellence

Our Story

For over 20 years, Electricians-SA & Plumbers has been the trusted choice for electrical and plumbing services across Gauteng. What started as a small team of passionate electricians has grown into a comprehensive service provider with a stellar reputation for reliability, quality, and customer care.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional electrical and plumbing services that exceed customer expectations while maintaining the highest safety and quality standards. We believe that every customer deserves professional, courteous service delivered by certified experts.

Our Values

  • Safety First: All work complies with South African electrical and plumbing safety regulations
  • Quality Workmanship: We stand behind every job with professional certifications
  • Customer Focus: Your satisfaction is our priority, with 24/7 support
  • Integrity: Transparent pricing with no hidden fees
  • Reliability: Emergency response 365 days a year
