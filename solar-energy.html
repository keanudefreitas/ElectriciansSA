System Types

Grid-Tied Systems

Connected to the municipal grid. Sell excess power back and buy when needed. Lowest cost option for day-time savings.

Hybrid Systems

Grid-connected with battery backup. Store excess power and use during peak times or loadshedding hours.

Off-Grid Systems

Complete independence from the grid with battery storage. Ideal for areas with unreliable power supply.
